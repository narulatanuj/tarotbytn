About Myself

A few years ago I attended a psychic and mediumship seminar in Melbourne, Australia, to observe a Medium work, and from that day I knew this was one of the things what I wanted to do with my life. This was the beginning of my pathway to becoming a Tarot and Psychic reader.

Over the years that followed, whilst learning and further developing at the Arthur Findlay College of Spiritual Science, I have taken every opportunity to develop and demonstrate my skills, intending to reunite relatives with their departed loved ones or to give you insights on your future.

So, let me tell you what I do – the ability I have is to make contact with loved ones who have died. That’s how this started for me. That contact can be through one or more of the following senses - seeing, hearing, feeling or just knowing. The second faculty, commonly known as the psychic dimension, that opens is my ability to read you – and that’s looking at the auric field and the energy that surrounds you. It gives me an opportunity to see the Architect’s plan of the soul – not the conscious mind, but of the soul and sometimes the two can be very different. Whilst the insights I give are based on the current life path you are on, please always remember life, at the end of the day, will depend on the choices you make – that's your free will or self-governance at work.
