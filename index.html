About Myself

A few years ago TJ attended a psychic and mediumship seminar in Melbourne, Australia, to observe a Medium work, and from that day TJ knew this was one of the things he wanted to do with his life. That was the beginning of his pathway to becoming a Psychic Medium.

Once TJ begun to consciously develop his Mediumship, TJ was quickly led to the Arthur Findlay College (London), the world’s foremost college for the Advancement of Mediumship and Psychic Sciences, where he embarked on an intensive study and development of his mediumistic abilities. It was here that he met his mentor, one of the finest teachers and exponents of Mediumship, who has significantly influenced TJ’s mediumistic development.

TJ has built a solid foundation based on the quality and accuracy of his work, his compassion, and the demonstration style he is unfolding.

So, let me tell you what I do....

The ability I have is to make contact with loved ones who have died. That’s how this started for me. That contact can be through one or more of the following senses - seeing, hearing, feeling or just knowing. The second faculty, commonly known as the psychic dimension, is my ability to read you – and that’s looking at the auric field, the chakras and the energy fields. It gives me an opportunity to see the Architect’s plan of the soul – not the conscious mind, but of the soul and sometimes the two can be very different. It is on this soul plan, your life path, that my honest insights are based on. A reading with me is a three way process involving the spirit world, the medium and you.
